Board Dashboard Templates

Three approaches for Charlie's weekly update. Pick one.

Option 1

Executive Cards

Metric tiles at top, expandable sections below. Clean, fast to scan. Best for a board chair who wants headlines first, detail on demand.

 Option 2

Narrative Flow

Story-driven. Larger typography, fewer numbers on screen at once. Each section tells a story with data alongside. Best for investors who want context with numbers.

 Option 3

Data Dense

Everything visible at a glance. Multi-column, compact tables, KPI sparklines. Best for operators who want to see the whole board at once.

 Option 4

Brand Narrative

Bookmarked brand-first. Rust and teal palette, DM Serif headlines, feels like the product itself. Best if this dashboard becomes a recurring artifact Charlie associates with the brand.
